Overview of Services

  1. Disease Dashboard : Explore our Disease Dashboard to understand various health conditions visually. We've organized them into categories like heart health, respiratory issues, mental wellness, and more. Each condition comes with a simple description to help you better understand it.
  2. Enter Test Results : Keep track of your health by entering your test results manually. Whether it's your blood pressure, blood sugar levels, or cholesterol levels, our platform allows you to input this data easily. We'll even provide graphical representations of your condition over time to help you track your progress.
  3. Person Condition Representation : Visualize your health condition through easy-to-understand diagrams and charts. Our platform translates your test results into graphical representations, making it simple to grasp your current health status at a glance.
  4. Precautions/Solutions : Discover practical tips and precautions tailored to your specific health condition. Whether it's managing diabetes, controlling high blood pressure, or coping with anxiety, we offer personalized recommendations to help you stay healthy and happy.
  5. Contact and Support : Need assistance or have questions? Our team is here to help. Feel free to reach out to us for support or additional information. We're committed to providing you with the help you need to navigate your health journey successfully.
