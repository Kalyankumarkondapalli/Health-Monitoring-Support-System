Precautions/Solutions

  • Blood Test: Regular check-ups, balanced diet, adequate hydration.
  • Urinalysis Test: Maintain a balanced diet, proper hydration, monitor sugar intake.
  • ECG Test: Regular exercise, avoid stress, regular medical check-ups.
  • Cholesterol Test: Healthy diet low in saturated fats, regular exercise, avoid smoking.
  • UltraSound Test: Follow doctor's advice for any abnormalities, regular screenings.
  • Blood Pressure Test: Low-salt diet, regular exercise, avoid excessive alcohol consumption, stress management.
